◆ USBDevicePath

typedef std::string leanux::usb::USBDevicePath

Typedef for usb device absolute paths.

When connected to a PCI bus, such a path may be '/sys/devices/pci0000:00/0000:00:1a.0/usb5/5-1'.

See also
enumUSBDevices to obtain valid device paths for all connected USB devices.
