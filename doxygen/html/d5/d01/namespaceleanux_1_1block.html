Detailed Description

block device API.

Block devices are represented by instances of the MajorMinor class, of which the member functions retrieve per-block device statistics. Block devices present on the system are enumerated into lists of MajorMinor objects with one of the enum* functions, such as enumWholeDisks(std::list< MajorMinor > &devices).
